Call for Proposals

We invite proposals for the following:

20 Minute Presentations

During the main conference session on September 13th, there will be a series of 20 minute long presentations. These presentation should convey an extended story about data.

5 Minute Lightning Talks

During both the party on September 12th and the main conference session on September 13th, there will be a series of 5 minute long lightning talks. These talks should introduce an idea or tell an anecdote about data.

40 Minute Panels

During the main conference on September 13th, there will be several extended panels. The panels are meant to spark discussions and debates among participants and the audience. A proposal for a panel should list both the topic of debate and the proposed participants in the panel.

Submissions

We consider your submission to be an expression of interest. Once we know what you’re hoping to say, we'll work with you to craft the best presentation format to tell your story. Also, if you have more than one interesting story to tell, please feel free to submit multiple proposals.

All submissions are due by May 17th, but earlier submissions will get precedence as part of a rolling acceptance policy. If you want to ensure consideration, please submit your proposal as soon as possible.
