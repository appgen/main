Is this your year to speak at
Strata Conference + Hadoop World?


The best minds in data will gather again in New York as Strata + Hadoop World returns—to learn, connect, and explore the issues and opportunities in big data. Since joining forces last year, Strata + Hadoop World is also one of the largest gatherings of the Apache Hadoop community, with emphasis on hands-on and business sessions on the Hadoop ecosystem.

Strata sells out every year. One reason for Strata's immense popularity is our speakers, who have real-world experience with exciting innovations in big data—and the desire to pass on their hard-won knowledge. Does that sound like you?

Whether you're a new or seasoned speaker—if you have a great idea, compelling case study (success stories as well as lessons learned), proven best practices, new approaches, or a talent for passing on technical skills, we'll put you in front of an intelligent and engaged audience and let you make your mark.

The breadth of attendees is a testament to the breadth of the conference, and the synergies at the conference were nearly visible in the air.
— Andrew Caird, University of Michigan

We're especially interested in:

  • Data: Distributed and real-time data processing, data acquisition and cleaning, data science best practices, machine learning, cloud platforms and infrastructure
  • Business: From research to product, becoming a data-driven organization, training and recruiting for data, the changing role of business intelligence
  • Design: Dashboards, mobile strategy, applications & futures, visualization and design principles, augmented reality and immersive interfaces

Submit your proposal by May 16 and save the dates for Strata + Hadoop World, happening October 28-30 at the New York Hilton.
